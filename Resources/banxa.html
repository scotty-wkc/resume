Customer Experience Analyst - Banxa (December '21 - June '22

My experience at Banxa was cut short due to the massive layoffs the crypto trading company went through in 2022 as a result of the crypto market crash. However, in this time, I gained valuable experience around process improvements, project management and stakeholder management on a global scale.

  • An example of process improvement at Banxa can be seen in my first task at Banxa, which involved migrating a 100+ page document that held key information and processes for our worldwide customer-facing teams into comprehensive and user- friendly process streams. The end-goal was to construct a KMS in Confluence. This included maintaining process modifications and teaching over 40 employees how to use the tool.
  • In the migration, I identified a hole in the initial response time of up to three to five working days for client questions. Using Confluence, I created a configurable chatbot that communicated with all customer-facing online and mobile apps under Banxa's authority. Before connecting to a real customer support agent, this bot would respond to enquiries from customers using a series of question alternatives. First contact time was cut from up to five business days to less than 20 minutes as a result.
  • I assembled a senior representative task force to continue refining procedures for an improved customer experience. Training them to go through the procedures in Confluence and determine how they may be optimized and updated while also continuously improving the chatbot.
