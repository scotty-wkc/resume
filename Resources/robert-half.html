Senior Recruiter - Robert Half (July '22 - Current)

Working at Robert Half has been a fantastic opportunity for me to develop my skills as a recruiter as well as exercise my ability to learn on-the-go and be adaptable to new environments. In this role, I worked primarily in recruiting for technology, an industry I had no prior knowledge of. I quickly learned the basics of the highly specialized technology industry while immersing myself fully in a fast-paced sales environment. My main duties included using tools like LinkedIn Recruiter, Seek Talent and/or inventory recruitment through Salesforce to identify top talent and ascertain whether the skillsets listed fit with the role requirements.

  • Through establishing robust and mutually advantageous connections with significant stakeholders in my target accounts, I successfully secured the participation of numerous sizable corporations as purchasers, including Iress, AHPRA, and Viva Energy Australia.
  • Engaging and advocating for my candidates to potential employers.
  • Unpacking job briefs with my clients to identify the underlying wants and needs outside of a standard job description.
  • Conducting reference checks, adhering to the clients onboarding process and walking the candidate through step-by-step to ensure a seamless transition between workplaces and roles.
  • Utilizing recruitment data from Seek and internal systems to provide trends and market insights to prospective clients and candidates.
  • Managing client engagement by establishing a ‘plan to placement’ allowing me to impact expectations across the board and raise placement chances from 15% to >80%.
  • Mentoring new hires within the technology recruitment team.
